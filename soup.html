Soup

Description

Soup is a liquid dish typically made by boiling meat, vegetables, or legumes in water or broth. It is a comforting meal that can be enjoyed hot or cold, often served as a starter or a main course.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables (carrot, onion, celery, etc.)
  • Broth (chicken, beef, or vegetable)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Herbs (parsley, thyme, bay leaves, etc.)
  • Garlic
  • Protein (chicken, beef, or tofu - optional)
  • Oil or butter

Steps

  1. Heat oil or butter in a pot and saut√© garlic and vegetables.
  2. Add broth and bring to a boil.
  3. Simmer until vegetables are tender.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and herbs.
  5. Add protein if desired and cook until done.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
