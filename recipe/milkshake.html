Milkshake

Descripion

A milkshake is a sweet, creamy drink made by blending milk, ice cream, and flavorings like chocolate or fruit.

Ingrediants:

  • milk
  • sugar
  • flavour
  • ice cream

Steps:

  1. Add milk and ice cream to a blender.
  2. Add your desired flavor (e.g., chocolate syrup, fruits).
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and serve.
