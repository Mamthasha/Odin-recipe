Noodles

Description

Noodles are a type of long, thin pasta made from flour, water, and sometimes eggs, widely enjoyed in various cuisines. They can be boiled, stir-fried, or served in soups, and are often paired with sauces, vegetables, or meats for a flavorful dish.

Ingrediants

  • Noodles
  • Vegetables (carrot, bell peppers, cabbage, etc.)
  • Garlic and ginger (minced)
  • Soy sauce
  • Chili sauce (optional)
  • Protein (meat, tofu, or egg - optional)
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles and drain.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and ginger.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender.
  4. Mix in soy sauce and chili sauce.
  5. Add noodles and toss to combine.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
